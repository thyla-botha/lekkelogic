Other capabilities For existing clients · On request

Still delivered.
No longer headline.

Five practices we still run, but no longer lead with. They sit alongside the seven core services for bundled engagements and existing client work. If you need any of these on their own, just ask on the call.

01

Managed IT.

Per-user IT support, helpdesk, endpoint management, and infrastructure operations for SME teams up to mid-market firms.

  • Basic IT support: R100 to R500 per user, per month
  • Larger business IT packages: R25k to R95k per month
  • Cloud, identity, and endpoint hygiene
  • Bundled with custom software builds where it makes sense
02

Business Development.

Go-to-market strategy, sales-system setup, partnerships, and fractional BD leadership for founders who need pipeline before they need polish.

  • Go-to-market strategy: R15k to R200k per project
  • Sales system / CRM setup: R10k to R180k per project
  • Partnership development: R12.5k to R150k+ per engagement
  • Fractional BD lead: R10k to R120k per month
03

Advertising.

Paid social, Google Ads / SEM, creative production, and full-service ad retainers. Management fee and ad spend always billed as separate line items.

  • Paid social management: R5k to R75k per month (spend extra)
  • Google Ads / SEM: R5k to R60k per month (spend extra)
  • Creative and ad production: R5k to R100k+ once-off
  • Full-service retainer: R15k to R200k+ per month (spend extra)
04

E-Commerce builds.

Shopify, headless commerce on Next.js, or custom builds. Catalogues, payment integration, and order operations. Usually bundled with Custom AI Software or Marketing Sites.

  • Lean builds: R30k to R250k
  • Premium builds: R150k to R1.5M+
  • Payment integration (PayFast, Yoco, Stripe)
  • Order ops, inventory, and post-launch support
05

Generic CMS / Website platforms.

WordPress, Webflow, and headless CMS work for clients with existing platforms. We prefer to ship Marketing Sites on Next.js, but we'll work where you are.

  • Lean rebuilds: R15k to R150k
  • Premium platforms: R80k to R600k+
  • Theme, plugin, and content modelling
  • Migration from legacy CMS
